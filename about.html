Our story

Dallas's Go-To Electricians Since 2008

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services that exceed customer expectations, while building lasting relationships in our community.

TX Dallas Electrics was founded by master electrician John Martinez after years of seeing homeowners and small businesses struggle to find trustworthy electricians. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to quality, John built a team of like-minded professionals who share his passion for craftsmanship and customer service. Today, we serve hundreds of clients across Dallas, from historic homes in M Streets to modern offices in the Design District.

Our growth has been driven by word-of-mouth and a simple philosophy: treat every home like our own. We invest in ongoing training, top-tier tools, and rigorous safety protocols. Whether it's a small repair or a large-scale installation, our team approaches every job with the same dedication to excellence. We're proud to be a local business that gives back and supports our neighbors.
