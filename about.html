Our story

Dallas’s Most Trusted Electrical Team

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services to every Dallas homeowner and business, with a commitment to quality and customer care.

TX Dallas Electrics was founded in 2012 by a team of master electricians who saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in the Dallas area. Starting with a single truck, we built a reputation for showing up on time, explaining every step, and delivering flawless results. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service electrical company serving thousands of satisfied customers across the metroplex.

Our team continues to invest in the latest training and technology to stay ahead of industry standards. From smart home integration to energy-efficient upgrades, we bring modern solutions to every project. But our core values remain the same: treat every home like our own, communicate clearly, and never cut corners.
