Panel Upgrades & Electrical Service

Upgrade your electrical panel for safety and capacity.

Schedule this service

Overview

Older panels can be a fire risk and may not support modern appliances. We specialize in replacing outdated fuse boxes and upgrading to 200-amp or higher service. Our team handles everything from permit acquisition to final inspection.

What's included

  • Increased capacity for new appliances or additions
  • Enhanced safety with arc-fault breakers
  • Free consultation and estimate

How it works

1

Assessment

We evaluate your current panel and load requirements to recommend the right upgrade.

2

Installation

Our electricians safely disconnect and replace the panel, rewiring as needed.

3

Inspection

We coordinate with the city for final inspection and ensure full code compliance.
