Panel Upgrades & Electrical Service

Upgrade your electrical panel for safety, capacity, and modern home needs.

Schedule this service

Overview

An outdated electrical panel can be a safety hazard and limit your home’s ability to handle modern appliances. Our panel upgrade services ensure your system meets current electrical codes and provides enough capacity for your needs. We handle everything from 100-amp to 400-amp upgrades.

What's included

  • Increased electrical capacity for new appliances
  • Enhanced safety with modern breakers
  • Compliance with local codes
  • Improved home value and peace of mind

Common questions

How do I know if I need a panel upgrade?

Signs include frequent breaker trips, flickering lights, or using extension cords for major appliances. Call us for an assessment.

How long does a panel upgrade take?

Most residential upgrades are completed in one day, depending on the complexity.
