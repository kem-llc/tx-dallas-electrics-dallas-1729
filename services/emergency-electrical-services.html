Overview

Electrical emergencies can happen anytime. Our team is on standby 24/7 to address power outages, exposed wires, sparking outlets, and other hazards. We prioritize safety and work quickly to restore your power and peace of mind.

What's included

  • Fast response within 60 minutes
  • No overtime or holiday surcharges
  • Thorough diagnostics and lasting repairs
  • Friendly, professional technicians

How it works

1

2

Common questions

What qualifies as an electrical emergency?

Any situation that poses a safety risk: sparking outlets, burning smells, power outages affecting only your home, or exposed wires.

Do you charge extra for after-hours calls?

No. We believe emergencies shouldn't cost extra, so our rates are the same 24/7.
