Common questions

When should I consider rewiring my home?

If your home is over 40 years old, has aluminum wiring, or you frequently trip breakers, it's time for an inspection.

Will rewiring damage my walls?

We minimize damage by using existing pathways when possible. Any necessary openings are patched and prepped for painting.
