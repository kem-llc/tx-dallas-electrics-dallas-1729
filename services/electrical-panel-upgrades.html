Overview

Older panels can't support modern appliances and electronics, leading to frequent tripping or hazards. We upgrade to high-capacity panels that improve safety, prevent overloads, and support future additions like EV chargers or solar.

What's included

  • Reduces risk of electrical fires
  • Increases property value and insurance savings

Common questions

When should I upgrade my electrical panel?

If you have an old fuse box, a 60-amp panel, or frequently trip breakers, it's time for an upgrade. Also consider upgrading before major renovations.

Will an upgrade increase my electric bill?

No, an upgrade itself doesn't increase usage. In fact, modern panels can improve efficiency and reduce energy waste.
